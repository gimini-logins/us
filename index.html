How to Create a Gemini Exchange Login Account?

It is very easy to create a Gemini account. Follow the below-mentioned steps to create a new Gemini account:

  • Visit Gemini official site in your browser, i.e., https://exchange.gemini.com/signin, or launch its mobile app.
  • Now, navigate to the sign-up page.
  • Then, provide all the details appearing on the Gemini wallet sign-up page.
  • Next, you need to create a strong password for your account using the password manager option.
  • At last, complete the email verification process. For this, go to the registered email account and follow the instructions that appear on the screen.

After successful email verification, wait till your account gets activated. Once done, you can use your Gemini login account to trade Bitcoin.

How do I Sign In for my Gemini Account?

If you have to access the Gemini account for the first time, follow the below-given Gemini sign in steps:

  • Firstly, launch a web browser in your system and go to the official Gemini webpage, i.e., https://www.gemini.com/.
  • Click on the “Sign in” icon located in the upper right corner.
  • Enter the email address and password that you have provided while setting up your account.
  • Now, click the “Sign in” button.
  • Also, provide all the details asked in any other fields that appear on-screen.

To access your Gemini account on your mobile, download and install the application on your device. After installing the app, enter your email address and password and go through the on-screen instructions to complete the sign-in process.

How to Reset Gemini Wallet Password?

Instances may occur when you get logged out of your Gemini account. In such cases, you need to go through the Gemini wallet password reset process to get access to your account. Follow the below-mentioned recovery steps to change the password of your Gemini wallet:

  • Visit https://www.gemini.com/ and click on the “Account” button located in the upper-right corner. 
  • Locate and click the “Security Settings” button. 
  • At last, tap the “Change Password” button from the “Password” section.
  • Enter a new password for your account. Make sure your password is as per the security norms. 

The above Gemini account recovery steps will help you to change the password for your account on both the app and the web. Once you complete the above steps, wait for 72-hour for any digital asset withdrawals.
